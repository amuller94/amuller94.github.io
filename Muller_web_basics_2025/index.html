This is a website about my Dungeons & Dragons campaign, because it's one of my favorite things in the world and I have a captive audience!

My friends Sarah, Leon, Maggie, and I started a game of D&D in November of 2019. We had all been listening to "actual play" shows, where a cast plays a tabletop roleplaying game (TTRPG) for an audience, and felt inspired to try ourselves. On November 20 of that year, we sat down at Sarah and Leon's apartment to create characters.

We were playing a prewritten module called Waterdeep Dragon Heist, for levels 1-5. In about a year and a half, we had finished the module and wanted to keep going. Maggie, who was DMing (running the game) for us, wrote some homebrew material in a fey setting. We leveled up to 6 and kept going. By then, we'd pretty much decided we wanted to take these characters all the way through to level 20, the highest that characters can go.

We are now level 17. The end is almost in sight, and we're all feeling pretty dang sentimental about it. Hence this project.
